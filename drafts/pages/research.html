Research

This page is a draft

My current research interests focus primarily on general relativity and geometry, especially where they intersect with gravitational waves. I have previously studied non-relativistic astrophysical phenomena such as AGN and dark matter clustering, and continue to be interested in the intersection of these phenomena in the context of relativity.
